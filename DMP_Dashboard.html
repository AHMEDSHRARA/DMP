Contractual Terms
DESIGN FREEZE
Design must be frozen at an agreed point in time to meet Programme milestones and submission dates. Required to activate infrastructure Concept Design for Phase 1A, 1B & 4 by August 2026.
ALIGNMENT GATEWAY
Formal touch-points to capture and confirm required decisions, client inputs, or data dependencies. Embedded at critical programme junctures.
MILESTONE DELAY DAMAGES
Daily damages apply if Milestones not achieved by due date. Maximum capped at 10% of total Price. Consultant must immediately notify Client of any slippage.
PROGRAMME UPDATES
Consultant submits updated programme weekly. Client reviews within 15 days. Monthly cut-off date: 25th of each month. Primavera P6 (XER format) required.
